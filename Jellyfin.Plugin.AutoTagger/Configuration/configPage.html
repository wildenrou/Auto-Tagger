Auto Tagger

Automatically applies genre, decade, rating, language and resolution tags to your movies and series. Works hand-in-hand with Seasonal Visibility.

General

Removes tags previously applied by Auto Tagger before reapplying.

Seasonal Tags

Tags items based on their premiere/release month.

Default: season-spring | Months default: 3,4,5
Default: season-summer | Months default: 6,7,8
Default: season-fall | Months default: 9,10,11
Default: season-winter | Months default: 12,1,2

Genre Tags

One mapping per line: Genre Name=tag-name. Case-insensitive.

Decade Tags

Tags items based on their release year.

Rating Tags

One mapping per line: OfficialRating=tag-name. e.g. PG-13=tag-rated-pg13

Language Tags

One mapping per line: iso639code=tag-name. e.g. eng=tag-lang-english

Resolution Tags

Tags items based on video stream height.
